GreenTech - Brand Identity

Project Overview

GreenTech is a sustainable technology startup that needed a complete brand identity system to establish their presence in the eco-friendly tech market and communicate their values effectively.

Challenge

The company lacked a cohesive brand identity that could effectively communicate innovation while emphasizing environmental responsibility and trustworthiness.

Solution

Developed a comprehensive brand identity system including logo, color palette, typography, and brand guidelines that balance modernity with sustainability values.

Brand Elements

Logo Design

Clean, modern logo combining technology and nature elements with sustainable color palette.

Visual System

Cohesive visual language including patterns, iconography, and imagery style guidelines.

EduFlow - Learning Platform

Project Overview

EduFlow is an innovative online learning platform designed to enhance student engagement through interactive content, personalized learning paths, and collaborative features.

Challenge

Traditional online learning platforms had low completion rates and poor user engagement due to monotonous interfaces and lack of interactivity.

Solution

Designed an engaging, gamified learning experience with progress tracking, interactive elements, and social learning features to increase motivation and completion rates.

Key Features

Personalized Learning

Adaptive learning paths based on individual progress and learning preferences.

Interactive Content

Engaging multimedia content with quizzes, simulations, and hands-on exercises.
