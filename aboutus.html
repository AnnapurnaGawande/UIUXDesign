My Story

From curiosity to passion, here's how I became a UI/UX designer

The Beginning

My journey started with a simple curiosity about how digital products work. I was fascinated by the seamless experiences that great apps and websites provided, and I wanted to understand the magic behind them.

Discovery

I discovered that behind every great digital experience was thoughtful design and user research. This realization led me to dive deep into the world of UI/UX design, learning about user psychology, design principles, and interaction patterns.

Professional Growth

Over the years, I've worked with startups, established companies, and everything in between. Each project taught me something new about users, business needs, and the delicate balance between aesthetics and functionality.
