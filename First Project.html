Fitness Tracker App

A mobile-first design that empowers users to stay consistent with their health goals through intuitive UI and engaging features.

Project Overview

The goal was to create a user-friendly fitness app that motivates users to track workouts, monitor progress, and build healthy habits. The solution included gamified progress, personalized dashboards, and dark/light theme support.

  • Platform: iOS & Android
  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator
  • Timeline: 6 Weeks

Design Process

1. Research & Discovery

Conducted interviews with 10 fitness app users to identify pain points. The top findings were poor habit tracking, confusing UI, and lack of reminders.

2. Wireframing

Sketched key user flows including onboarding, tracking, and progress screens. Created low-fidelity wireframes in Figma for quick feedback.

3. High-Fidelity Design

Built a clean and modern UI using a calming color palette and bold typography. Used components for consistency across 30+ screens.

4. Testing & Iteration

Usability testing with 5 users revealed friction in progress navigation. Iterated with better icons, progress rings, and simplified layout.

Project Results

After launch, the app saw a 40% increase in daily active users and a 22% improvement in goal completion rate. Users praised the clean interface and motivation features.
