FreshMart – Smart Grocery Shopping App

A UX case study redesigning a grocery shopping app to reduce forgotten items, improve product discovery, and enhance personalization.

Project Overview

This 4-week redesign aimed to solve poor product discovery and forgotten shopping items. The improved UX features AI-powered lists, visual search, and better delivery flow.

  • Platform: Mobile App
  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Maze, UserInterviews
  • Duration: 4 Weeks

Problem & User Pain Points

Users struggled with forgotten items (72%), product search (68%), and poor UX causing cart abandonment (45%). Traditional apps lacked personalization and flexibility.

User Persona

Priya (29, Working Professional): Needs quick, efficient shopping. Struggles with product search and planning. Prioritizes delivery reliability and budget control.

Design Solutions

  • AI-Powered Lists: Smart suggestions, voice input, and store layout-based categorization.
  • Enhanced Discovery: Visual search, dietary filters, and recipe-based shopping.
  • Intelligent Delivery: Live tracking, smart substitutions, and flexible 30-min time slots.

Key Features

  • Clean, card-based interface with one-tap reordering
  • Offline access to shopping lists
  • Progressive loading for low network areas

Results & Impact

  • 65% faster shopping list creation
  • 40% fewer forgotten items
  • 30% increase in repeat usage
  • User satisfaction rating: 4.6/5
