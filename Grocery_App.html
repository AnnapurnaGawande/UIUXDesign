FreshMart – Smart Grocery Shopping

Revamping the grocery shopping experience with AI, intuitive design, and user-centric flows.

Project Snapshot

  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Duration: 4 Weeks
  • Platform: Mobile App
  • Tools: Figma, Maze, UserInterviews

Challenge

Poor product discovery, high cart abandonment, and inefficient list building were key hurdles. The app lacked personalization and smart assistance.

Design Process

Empathize

Conducted 10 user interviews and identified common frustrations with shopping apps, focusing on forgetfulness, poor discovery, and time delays.

Define

Mapped pain points and created personas like Priya. Defined core goals: speed, personalization, and reliability.

Ideate

Brainstormed AI-based solutions, delivery optimizations, and visual enhancements. Sketched multiple wireframe concepts.

Prototype

Built interactive low and mid-fidelity wireframes using Figma. Iterated based on peer feedback and usability heuristics.

Test

Ran usability tests with 8 participants. Focused on task success, clarity, and satisfaction. Improved flow and touch targets.

Implement

Final designs reflect tested ideas and real user needs. Handoff files prepared for future developer collaboration.

User Persona

Priya, 29 – A busy professional who values time, efficient planning, and dependable deliveries.

Top Pain Points

  • Forgets items during shopping
  • Struggles to find dietary-based products
  • Dislikes long checkout times

Design Solutions

AI Lists

Voice-based input, auto-categorization, and smart suggestions simplify shopping.

Product Discovery

Visual search and dietary filters help find exact items quickly and easily.

Smart Delivery

Live tracking, time slots, and substitutions build trust and reliability.

Outcome & Impact

  • 65% faster list creation
  • 40% fewer forgotten items
  • 4.6/5 user satisfaction
  • 30% increase in repeat usage

Future Improvements

  • Add smart voice assistant integration for hands-free shopping
  • Include loyalty rewards for frequently purchased items
  • Enable in-app barcode scanning for quick additions
  • Gamify grocery planning with challenges and weekly goals
